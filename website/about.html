Our Story

D. Majumdar & Associates was founded with a singular vision: to provide exceptional legal services that protect and advance our clients' business interests. Established in Kolkata, our firm has grown to become a trusted name in indirect tax litigation and commercial law.

Over the past decade, we have successfully represented clients across India in complex tax disputes, commercial arbitrations, and corporate matters. Our team's deep expertise and commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of businesses ranging from emerging enterprises to established corporations.

Today, we continue to uphold the highest standards of legal practice, combining traditional values of integrity and diligence with modern approaches to complex legal challenges.

Meet Our Founder

Dipankar Majumdar is the founder and principal advocate of D. Majumdar & Associates. With over 10 years of experience in legal practice, he has established himself as a leading authority in indirect tax litigation and commercial law.

His expertise spans across GST, Customs, Service Tax, and Entry Tax matters, with extensive experience appearing before CESTAT, GST Appellate Tribunal, and various High Courts across India.

Mr. Majumdar is known for his strategic approach to complex legal challenges, combining thorough legal research with practical business understanding to deliver favorable outcomes for his clients.
