Indirect Tax Litigation & Advisory

Our firm has extensive experience in handling complex indirect tax matters across all major tribunals and courts in India. We represent clients in disputes involving GST, Customs, Service Tax, and Entry Tax before CESTAT, GST Appellate Tribunal, and various High Courts.

Our services include:

  • GST litigation and advisory
  • Customs duty disputes and classification matters
  • Service Tax appeals and refund claims
  • Entry Tax and VAT litigation
  • Anti-dumping and safeguard duty matters
  • Tax planning and compliance advisory
